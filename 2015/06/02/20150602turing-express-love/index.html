图灵遇到危险，劝说未婚妻离开自己。

——I’m a homesexal

——all right

——men, not women

——so what?

I had my suspicions, I always did. But we’re not like other people. We love each other in our own way, and we can have the life together that we want. You won’t be the perfect husband. I have no intention of being the perfect wife. I’ll not be …fixing your lamb all day while you come from the office. I’ll work. You’ll work. And we’ll have each other’s company. We’ll have each other’s minds. That sounds like a better marriage than most. Because I care for you. And you care for me. And we understand one another more than… more than anyone else ever has.

——I don’t.

——what?

——Care for you.I never did. I just needed you to break Enigma(德国通讯的秘密系统). I’ve done that now, so…so you can go.

——I’m not going anywhere. I’ve spent entirely too much my life worried about what you think of me, or my parents think of me, or the boys in Hut 8 or the girls in Hut 3. And do you know what? I’m done. This is the most important work I will ever do, and no one is going to stop me. Least of all, you. You know what, they are right. You really are a monster.
